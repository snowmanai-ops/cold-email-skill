## Cadence Timing

| Email | Day | Strategy |
|-------|-----|----------|
| #1 | 0 | A/B/C split ‚Äî send each variation to ~33% of list |
| #2 | 4 | Re-state value prop + add case study |
| #3 | 7 | Provide something tangible or redirect to another contact |
| #4 | 12 | Pattern interrupt (spy emoji only) |
| #5 | 33 | Soft re-engagement after long gap |

## Complete Example: TikTok Influencer Marketing

### Email #1 ‚Äî Day 0

Split into 3 variations, each sent to ~33% of the lead list.

**Variation A:**
```
Subject: Quick question

Hey {{firstName}},
I was checking out your website the other day - love your products!
Based on your website, I think your products would be a huge hit on TikTok.
Curious, are you looking to partner with 200 TikTok influencers each month to promote {{companyName}}'s products to their audiences?
```

**Variation B:**
```
Subject: Quick question

Hey {{firstName}},
Noticed you're in charge of {{companyName}}'s marketing - I think your products would go viral on TikTok.
We can help you generate more organic sales by partnering you with 200 TikTok influencers each month to promote your products to their audiences - just like we recently did for Fashion Nova.
Are you open to hearing how it works?
```

**Variation C:**
```
Subject: Quick question

Hey {{firstName}},
Was doing some shopping and came across {{companyName}}'s website - I think your products would be a hit on TikTok.
We can partner you with 200 influencers each month to promote your products to their audiences - all at no cost to you.

Open to learning how we can help you do the same?
```
